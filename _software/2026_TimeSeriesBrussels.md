This Kaggle project presents a traffic count dataset from the city of Brussels in Belgium. The dataset provides valuable insights into urban mobility patterns by recording vehicle counts across different locations and times. Our goal is to perform an initial exploration, identify data quality aspects such as missing values, and prepare the data for future analyses, such as traffic forecasting or network modeling. The dataset was obtained in collaboration with Macq under the research project [TORRES](https://torresml6.wordpress.com/) and any information that could be used to identify the sensors' location has been removed.

[See project](https://www.kaggle.com/code/am0rales2025/time-series-imputation-in-brussels-belgium)
